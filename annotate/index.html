annotate

Allows you to annotate text, intended for reviewing stories and the like. You can annotate text like [original text](note) or [original text](type|note). Supported types are content, form, grammar, spelling and tense. Use them as you see fit.

Paste your text into the field below and add your annotations. When you're done, copy the text out again! This page does not store anything.

Select some text and hit Alt + Shift + c/f/g/s/t to automatically insert the annotation.
